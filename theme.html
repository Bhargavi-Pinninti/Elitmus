Treasure Hunt in Haunted Mansion

You set foot in an abandoned mansion, the door is closed. It is gloomy, chill and soundless. You are hearing screams that " find or die".

You are welcome to play the game.Puzzles and riddles are given by the ghost in each floor to obstruct you from earning more.

Your mission is to cross each level by scoring more. The more you score... The more you earn!!!
